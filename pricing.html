Controlled platform access

Access is reviewed by project and role.

SHAFFAF is positioned as a controlled construction transparency platform for UAE off-plan projects. Developers, buyers, investors, and authority reviewers request access based on their role, documents, and verification needs.

Developer onboarding

Trade license, project registration, permit references, supplier chain, and update responsibility.

Buyer / investor access

Project-only access by floor and unit, signed documents, videos, inspection records, and handover status.

Authority review

Read-only oversight with audit trail, supplier sources, materials, video evidence, and document history.
